**Last Updated:** February 7, 2026

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly from our users. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### Types of Data Collected
*   **Usage Data:** We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used. This may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), device type, device ID, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.
*   **Cookies/Local Storage:** We use local storage (LocalStorage) on your device to save your game progress, high scores, collected stars, and system settings (language, sound). This data stays on your device.
